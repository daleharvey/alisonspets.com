Hi! I am Alison and I offer Dog Walking and Pet Sitting services in Fife and Dundee, let me tell you a little more about myself:

Since leaving the RAF in the ‘80s, I have had a varied collection of pets from dogs, cats, rabbits, a chinchilla and a cockatiel, all leading a full happy and healthy life. I currently have Indy (Border Collie), Bailey and Murphy my moggies or as I like to call them, my fur babies :-)

After a long career in Administration I have started to do full time what I have done for friends and family for many years, walking dogs and looking after pets.

Looking after other peoples’ pets is a lot of responsibility so I have taken the NarpsUK Business Course (National Association of Pet Sitting and Dog Walking) to update my skillset from a normal pet owner to a responsible and knowledgeable Pet Sitter / Dog Walker so that I may give you the best service possible, I also intend to extend this to dog training.

Feel free to browse my website and have a look at the testimonials and if you require any further information please do not hesitate to contact me.
